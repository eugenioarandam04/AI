      Purpose

      About Us

      This project was created to make legal and economic concepts easier to approach for both professionals and the wider public.

      "We are two developers, soon to be qualified lawyers, who identified a need for a system that assists legal professionals and the general public in better understanding their legal position in matters such as leases, credit arrangements, and default interest. We recognize that calculating and interpreting these elements can often be complex and challenging. In response, we developed an application designed to serve as a practical tool in this context. This application was created for educational purposes, with the aim of providing value from both a legal and economic perspective in relation to these matters."

      Leases

      Legally, a lease establishes rights and duties. Economically, it allocates risk, mobility, and payment pressure over time.

      Credit

      Loan agreements are contracts, but their long-term impact is economic. Interest structure can change whether a deal is useful or dangerous.

      Default Interest

      Default interest operates as both penalty and compensation. Knowing how it grows helps debtors prioritize action earlier.
